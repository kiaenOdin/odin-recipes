Salsa

Description

This is my family's "personal" salsa recipe. Orginally based on Ted's Mexican Resurants recipe.

Ingredients

  • 5 cans stewed tomatoes(drain cans)
  • 1 large white onion
  • 1 bulb of garlic
  • 2 habaneros
  • 4 jalepenos
  • 4 serrano
  • 1 bunch of cilantro
  • 2 lemons(juice)

The listed ingredients are a base amount, you may get extra for adjustments.

Steps

  1. Separate ingredients into 4 equal portions.
  2. In a food processor, add ingredients, and chop/blend the ingredients together.
  3. After each portion, taste test, and determine if you would like to use less or more of any ingredient
  4. Once you are happy with the taste, add to large bowl, and begin next portion
  5. When you have mixed all the portions together, taste test one last time, you can add some sugar if it's a bit too spicy, then separate into 5 portions, you can freeze 4, and keep the last to eat(If you know how to can things, salsa is a great thing to can instead of freeze).
