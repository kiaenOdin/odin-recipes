Instantpot Rice and Chicken

Description

This Spicy Instant Pot Chicken and Rice is inspired by the chicken burrito bowls from Chipotle! It's quick and easy to make and meal preps for five days!

The recipe is made in two steps! You prepare the first portion in a pressure cooker, and after it's done cooking, you add some extra ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 1 onion, chopped roughly
  • 3 chicken breasts(I use about 2 pounds of chicken)
  • 2 cups(8 ounces) chicken broth
  • 1 tbsp Garlic powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 500ml Salsa
  • Rice(white rice works best, but I prefer brown rice)
  • Corn, beans or anything else you would like to add
  • Cilantro or garnish of your choice

As with all recipes, adjust to your liking.

Steps

  1. (Optional)Marinade your chicken in your marinade of choice. I personally use a lemon garlic marinade.
  2. Place your chopped onion in the bottom of your pressure cooker.
  3. On top of the onion place your chicken.
  4. Sprinkle your spices on top of your chicken, followed by the salsa and rice. (Do not stir)
  5. Pour in your chicken broth, and pat down the ingredients to make sure everything is covered.
  6. Set your pressure cooker to 15-20 minutes depending on the thickness of your chicken
  7. While that is cooking prep your corn and beans
  8. Once the pressure cooker has finished, remove your chicken, and stir in all addtional ingredients
  9. Cut/shred/prep your chicken in your preferred way.(I use 1/2 of a chicken breast per serving)
  10. Add chicken and rice mixture to bowl, and save the rest if you are meal prepping.
  11. Add any garnish you would like to your bowl,(My girlfriend Margo says sourcream is a must) and enjoy!
